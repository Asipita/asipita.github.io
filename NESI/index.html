About Us
Navigators Energy Stakeholders Initiative is a Not-for-profit and Non-governmental Organization.
We are a group of dedicated professionals buoyed with a passion of ensuring that the rights of Electricity Consumers in Nigeria are preserved and protected.
Aims and Objectives
  • To enlighten consumers of electricity of their rights from the power utility companies
  • To enlighten consumers of electricityof their obligations to the power utility companies
  • To resolve issues relating to estimated billing / crazy billing churn out to the consumers by the power utility companies
  • To address reported cases of extortion of consumers by the power utility companies
  • To address issues relating to unncecessary downtime in fixing and restoration of power supply to consumers as a result of unplanned outages
  • To address complaints relating to delay in metering of consumers by the power utility companies
  • To address issues relating to safety of consumers of electricity by the power utility companies
  • To address other electric power related challenges facing consumers of electricity
